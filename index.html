July 5–6

Mehndi & my name

You went for mehndi; I slipped away to meet you. It was a mixed, tender feeling — writing my name into the art on your hands, already smelling of tomorrow’s vows.

A Monday for Shiv

We shifted the roka to Monday because we are both Shiv bhakt. Ankita had kept sixteen Somvaar vrat for a better husband — and somehow every delay before that day only made the arrival feel more certain.

Then we became us

Shopping, fittings, family plans, nerves, relief — all of it led here. The next morning was not only ceremony. It was the day our private story became a public promise.

Better Together

We cut the ceremony cake… and then the clock tipped into my birthday. Balloons, photographs, cake, a written note, flowers — a full surprise tray I never saw coming.

Photos, laughter, home

Birthday in Chandigarh

On July 7 you ordered white roses and a written note before we left. Street food, Sukhna again, and a long talk in the car — the first time I felt something deepen into certainty and started feeling for you. It was a feeling that I won't be able to describe in words but I will always remember it.

Alpha & a forehead kiss

On July 10 we spontaneously went for Alpha at Cosmos Mall, Zirakpur. I kissed your forehead for the first time — then dinner at Gola Sizzlers, your first beer in front of me, and a late scolding from our parents.

Ambala station

On July 12 I surprised you before your train to Noida — snacks for the journey, another handwritten note, and the smallest miniature glass bouquet.

“I miss you”
